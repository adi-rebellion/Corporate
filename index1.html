Jesus Journey

Jesus Journey

What was the first thought that struck you when someone said Jesus Journey? Think! In simple words it's nothing but to Journey with Him to the Father's House. Every journey isn't a cakewalk, you might have hardships that wreck you. But the journey of life started the moment you were out of your Mother's womb and it's still on. It may have wrecked you and might still be wrecking you. Here's what matters when you say Jesus Journey, not how you're journeying but who is journeying with you, not how big the obstacle in front of you is but how big your companion is in the journey and not how narrow your road is but who is walking with you in this. It's ultimately Jesus who walks you through every phase of your life. Journeying with Jesus is probably the most simple way because all we need to do is BELIEVE and Just BELIEVE. Pray and Believe, the only solution to any problem that looks big. The Jesus Journey has helped each one of us immensely and uniquely in wondrous ways, despite how flawed we were. We encourage you to read and be touched by testimonials in the Jesus Journey Testimonial section which boldly underlines the Deep Love of God.

Prepratory Prayer

Prepratory Prayer

Winning a soul for the Kingdom is not a one man show we believe, but when one soul receives Christ as his personal saviour there is celebration and rejoicing above the skies. It's beautiful how people in Christ come together to pray for the lost souls to experience that love which was revealed on the Cross where Jesus died for the sins of You and Me. Every individual needs the guidance of the Holy Spirit to win souls. Not by human strength but only the Strength that comes from above and the Power of His Blood. Every Saturday at 5:00 PM worship leaders, ushers, musicians, audio and video technicians join together for a time of worship and intercession. We pray and intercede for the guidance of the Holy Spirit during the worship, for the congregation to experience the touch of His Love and for the man of God who will be sharing the word that each one of them is Blessed. This has helped not only those on the pulpit but also helped us see a huge shift among the congregation. Expanding our Father's House is a great privilege. The House of the Lord is open to anyone. Come and be a part.

Who We Are


We at Corporate fellowship nurture and reach out to the young students with the Gospel who are lost, who don’t know their purpose in life, entangled in the web of unknown sins which ruins their life.
We share the immense love of Christ which can transform every individual into a fine being. We here at DoVE are completely dedicated to this service besides counselling the students with prayer, rebuilding their Identity with compassion and love. We commission them to be the Army for Christ who are ready anytime to share the Gospel.
